HorizonTag — the three funding horizons, near to far.

```jsx
<HorizonTag horizon="today" />
<HorizonTag horizon="tomorrow" />
<HorizonTag horizon="forever" />
```

`today` = current need, `tomorrow` = multi-year priority, `forever` = legacy. The icon goes from a full sun to a distant ring to carry distance. Use `solid` for high emphasis.
