RoleChip — the two roles that must never look alike. `manager` = ownership (the staff owner who carries the relationship); `partner` = leverage (the warm path who can open the door).

```jsx
<RoleChip role="manager" name="Dana Reese" />
<RoleChip role="partner" name="Tom Bradley" />
```

Manager renders as a solid, anchored blue chip with the owner's avatar; partner as a dashed sage chip leading with a door-opening glyph. Never restyle one to resemble the other.
